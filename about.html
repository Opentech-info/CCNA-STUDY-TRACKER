About Opentech-info

Connecting the world, one packet at a time.

Our Mission

At Opentech-info, our mission is to democratize networking education. We believe that everyone, regardless of their background, should have access to high-quality, practical, and engaging resources to build a successful career in technology. The CCNA Study Tracker is our first major step towards achieving this goal.

What is the CCNA Study Tracker?

This tool was born from a simple idea: studying for the CCNA should be organized, interactive, and efficient. We wanted a single, dynamic platform where students could:

  • Visually track their study progress across all CCNA topics using an intuitive Kanban board.
  • Instantly access a comprehensive and accurate database of Cisco IOS commands, complete with explanations and context.
  • Stay motivated with a clean, modern, and interactive user interface that makes studying less of a chore.

Who We Are

We are a small team of networking professionals and enthusiasts passionate about open-source technology and education. With real-world experience in enterprise networking, we understand the challenges and opportunities that come with a career in this field. This project is our way of giving back to the community that has given us so much.

Our Commitment to You

This project is built for you, the student. It is, and always will be, free to use. We are committed to keeping the command database up-to-date and continuously improving the tool based on your feedback. If you have any suggestions or find a bug, please don't hesitate to visit our GitHub repository or contact us.
