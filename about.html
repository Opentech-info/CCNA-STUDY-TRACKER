About Opentech-info

Connecting the world, one packet at a time.

Democratizing Networking Education

At Opentech-info, our mission is to make networking education accessible to everyone. We believe that high-quality, practical resources are the key to building a successful career in technology, and we're dedicated to creating tools that are both engaging and effective.

An Interactive Study Platform

This tool was born from a simple idea: studying for the CCNA should be organized and efficient. We wanted a dynamic platform where students could:

  • Visually track progress with an intuitive Kanban board.
  • Instantly access a comprehensive command database.
  • Stay motivated with a clean, modern interface.

Passionate Professionals

We are a small team of networking professionals and enthusiasts passionate about open-source technology and education. With real-world experience, we understand the challenges of this field and built this project to give back to the community.

Built For You

This project is, and always will be, free. We are committed to keeping the command database up-to-date and improving the tool based on your feedback. Have a suggestion? Contact us or visit our GitHub.
